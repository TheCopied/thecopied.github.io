hashtag metaverse



This album consists of 7 tracks with an accompanying assortment of 7 digital images. The images were all derived from a single stock photo of the keyboard used to make the sounds in the album -- a Leopold FC750R. These iterations were created in GIMP. These images seek to enhace or emphasize aspects of the photo, and through this process ultimately destroy the image.

The tracks were composed in FL Studio, and were made using a mixture of samples, software instruments, and plug-ins. Often audio files were exported to then be reused as samples, therefore increasing the scale of various effects. The speech was made using a free browser text-to-speech generator. The album was inspired by the metaverse entering public discussions and awareness following the creation of Meta from Facebook.

There is present in the album both a concern for and enjoyment of a sense of decay, both a fear and disgust at our potential trajectory towards virtuality and a sense that art is squeezed out of our terminal velocity.
